About The Event

Leave Your
Mark

Once, our ancestors huddled around a flame — it was the first technology. It changed everything. Now, we gather around screens and terminals, wielding a different kind of fire.

Dev Den is Bennett University's premier 24-hour hackathon — where chaos becomes creation, and great ideas get forged under pressure, the same way steel meets flame.
